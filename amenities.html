Amenities


Infinity Pool

Immerse yourself in luxury at our stunning infinity pool overlooking the mesmerizing sea view. Let the tranquil sound of waves and the gentle ocean breeze surround you as you relax in crystal-clear waters that seem to merge seamlessly with the horizon. Whether basking in the sun or enjoying a twilight swim, every moment here reflects pure coastal elegance.

Spa & Wellness

Rejuvenate your mind, body, and soul at our serene Spa & Wellness center. Surrounded by the calming sounds of the sea, indulge in soothing massages, refreshing therapies, and holistic treatments designed to restore balance and inner peace. Let every moment of care renew your energy and leave you glowing with pure tranquility.

Restaurant & Bar

Savor the taste of the coast at our ocean-view Restaurant & Bar. Enjoy a fine selection of local and international cuisines prepared with fresh ingredients and coastal flavors. As the sun sets, unwind with signature cocktails or a chilled drink by the sea breeze. Whether it’s a romantic dinner or a casual gathering, every meal here is served with style and a touch of paradise.

Water Sports

Dive into adventure with our thrilling Water & Sports activities by the sea. From jet skiing and snorkeling to paddleboarding and beach volleyball, there’s excitement for everyone. Feel the rush of the waves, embrace the ocean breeze, and make unforgettable memories under the sun. Whether you seek adventure or fun with friends, the sea is your playground.
